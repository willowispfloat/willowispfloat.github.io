YOVDA Inline joystick for Switch consoles with RGB Features:

Compatible with Nintendo Switch/OLED 

Black case design + 8 colors RGB breathing lights

Full-size handle grip and ergonomic design

3 Turbo acceleration, auto attack and back buttons

Dual motor vibration and 6-axis GYRO motion senser

Plug and play, no latency issues

1.Installation Steps:

1: Select "System Setup" from the main menu

2: Select "Controllers and Sensors

3: Turn on "Pro Controller Wired Communications

4: Plug the On/Off OLED Console into the controller to complete the connection.

2.Vibration Adjustment:

Enhance vibration via the right "lightning bolt icon" button

Reduce vibration with the left "lightning icon" button

Motor vibration is categorized into 5 stages: 100%, 75%, 50%, 30%, and Off.

3.TURBO function:

1. TURBO speed setting

A, 5 consecutive shots per second 1 speed

B, 12 consecutive shots per second 2 speeds

C, 20 consecutive shots per second 3 speeds

Adjustment: Press TURBO key and+key to increase the speed by one step;Press TURBO key and-key to decrease the speed by one step.

4. Turbo Setting:

Press for the first time the A/B/X/Y/L1/L2/RI/R2/Cross and Turbo buttons to set the Turbo function;

Press the TURBO key again second time without setting Turbo to realize the automatic turbo function;

Press the TURBO button again third time without setting Turbo to cancel the TURBO function;

Press and hold the Turbo button for 5 seconds to clear the Turbo function of all buttons; 

5. Lighting control:

Cool breathing light, 8 RGB colors

Care and Maintenance

Preserve the pristine beauty of your leather accessory by avoiding damp, unventilated spaces. Regular cleaning with a dry cloth is recommended. Avoid water exposure to maintain the integrity.

For stain management:
(1) Start by gently wiping the surface with a semi-dry cloth or wet wipe;
(2) Apply a small amount of colorless leather cleanser or oiled emollient product onto a clean cloth;
(3) Gently rub the cleanser into the leather, ensuring even coverage, and then allow it to dry naturally;
(4) Finish the process by briskly wiping with a soft, dry cloth, restoring it"s natural sheen and texture.

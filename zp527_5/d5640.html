How to connect a backlit keyboard
* Open the lid, turn on the keyboard
* Turn on the iPad"s BT
* Click the "Connect" key to turn on the keyboard"s BT
* Enter the iPad"s BT settings , click BT and look for "BT "Keyboard" in the search page, just select it

Note
1. Please charge the keyboard 2~3 times when using it for the first time Hours
2. Turn on the keyboard, the power indicator will flash for about 3 seconds and then turn off
3. When using it for the second time: just turn on the BT of the tablet and power on the keyboard, please press any key on the keyboard. Then it It will connect automatically
(no need to click "BT 3.0 keyboard" on the iPad screen, and don"t press the "CONNECT" button on the keyboard, otherwise it will show "Not connected")

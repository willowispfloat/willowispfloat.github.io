There are other variations and trademark names for high temperature glass. Pyraceram(tm) is one. I don"t have a list of them all. Regular window glass and safety glass expand too much from the grinding heat and will crack. I"ve learned this the hard way. There is an important caution you must be aware of when using Pyrex like this. This is NOT safety glass. If it shatters, it will produce very sharp and dangerous shards of glass. If it comes loose from your platen while grinding, it could be a disaster. Always wear eye protection when grinding. I have heard of several reports of glass liners coming off during grinding. The heat generated softens the adhesive and the glass gives way. In every case, the glass shattered and threw dangerous pieces of glass. There are plenty of reasons not to use this material on your grinder. Having said all that, I use it and have for years. I think most makers that try it, swear by it. You DO have to be careful when you apply it to your platen.

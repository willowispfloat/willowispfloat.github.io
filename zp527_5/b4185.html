This is more than Dual Karaoke Microphones with speakers. It is a versatile and portable PA system with MIC with Bluetooth capabilities that can be used as a stand alone or connect with a AUX Cable or USB Cable to other devices or run your device’s sound through the M-200, so compact but oh so versatile.

You receive two Microphones and one Base Set, perfect for Karaoke Duets or for Question and Answers from an audience. Connect to any sound system PA with our enclosed AUX Cable and fill the room with crystal clear sound.

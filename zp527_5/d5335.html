How to connect the keyboard with tablet?

1. please fully charge the keyboard and turn it on.

2. Press ‘connect’ button to open the keyboard’s bluetooth.

3. Go to the tablet settings, click on bluetooth and look for the keyboard.

4. Select the ‘bluetooth 3.0 keyboard’ and it will connect.

2. Turn on the keyboard, the power indicator will flash for about 3 seconds then goes off.

3. When using it for the second time: only need to turn on your tablet"s Bluetooth and keyboard power, press any key on the keyboard. Then it will automatically connect.

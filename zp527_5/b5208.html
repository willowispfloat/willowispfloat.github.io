How to connect the keyboard with your iPad?

2. Press the "connect" button on the right top corner of the keyboard to switch to the Bluetooth function

3. Turn on iPad"s Bluetooth, Search Bluetooth device on iPad

4. You will get 4 digital numbers on iPad

5. Use the keyboard to enter 4 digital number and then press "enter"

6. Step finished, keyboard and iPad matched

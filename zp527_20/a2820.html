How to Pair:

1. Plug the USB receiver into your device and wait for the LED to start flashing. Then press and hold the "SYNC" button on the receiver until it begins fast-blinking.

2. Locate the "Reset" button on the top of the controller and press it. The LED on the controller will start flashing.

3. Press the "Start" button and wait for the LED to stay lit, indicating that the controller is now connected.

Note Before Pairing:

1. Make sure the controller is fully charged.

2. Using on Switch. Please go to the System settings>Controllers and Sensors>Pro Controller Wired Communication>ON, the option should select "ON"

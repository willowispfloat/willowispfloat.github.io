How to connect guitar with Kithouse Guitar Headphone Amp?
The amplifiers have a jack that connects to the guitar directly, no cords required. On one side, it has one more 3.5mm(1/8”) port where you can plug your headphones or speakers(though D3.5 to D6.35 Audio line) into the latter directly.

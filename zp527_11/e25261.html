説明: 6 Port Rack Mounted midspan PoE injector for PoE applications. Use this Gigabit PoE midspan injector to add PoE to any switch. Remove clutter in your installations and extend power up to 328 feet (1 meter) away from the source. Use this device to replace up to 6 individual power over ethernet injectors and condense into one single gigabit POE module.

【本の内容】
Updated to keep pace with the many changes in the field Crowley"s An Introduction to Human Disease: Pathology and Pathophysiology Correlations Eleventh Edition provides readers with a clear well-illustrated explanation of the structural and functional changes associated with disease the clinical manifestations of disease and how to determine treatment. It reflects current information on the pathenogenesis of infectious disease and how changes in the genome are expressed as disease. The first chapters of the text discuss general concepts and diseases affecting the body as a whole. Later chapters consider the various organ systems and their diseases. The Eleventh Edition includes new content on COVID-19 Zika virus brain eating amoebas plus mini podcasts to address difficult concepts such as cancer and the use of microscope slides. Each new print copy includes Navigate Advantage Access that unlocks a comprehensive and interactive eBook with animations student practice activities and assessments a full suite of instructor resources and learning analytics reporting tools.
